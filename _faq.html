FAQ

1. What is The Cartel Marketplace?

The Cartel Marketplace is an online platform that connects buyers and sellers for a wide

range of quality products, from fashion to electronics, offering the best deals and a

seamless shopping experience.

2. How do I register on The Cartel?

To register, visit our registration page and fill out the required information. You will receive a

confirmation email once your registration is successful.

3. What payment methods are accepted?

We accept various payment methods, including credit cards, bank transfers, and e-wallets.

Please choose the method that suits you best during checkout.

4. How can I return a product?

If you are not satisfied with the product you received, you can request a return within 14

days of receiving the item. Please ensure the product is in its original condition and

complete.
